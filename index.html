Happy birthday, my love ❤️

I’m truly grateful that I got the chance to know you, to love you, and to be loved by you. You’ve brought so much happiness into my life, more than I could ever put into words. You make my days brighter, my heart lighter, and my life feel complete.

Thank you for being you. Thank you for choosing me.

I love you, always and forever. Happy birthday again, my future wife 💖
